About Me

Michelle Dinh is an aspiring web designer and developer majoring
in Digital Media Art at San Jose State University. Located in the heart of
Silicon Valley, she uses digital media and technologies to show how 2D and
3D spaces interact. As a boba enthusiast, she enjoys visiting new places and
sampling a whole range of drinks on her free time.
