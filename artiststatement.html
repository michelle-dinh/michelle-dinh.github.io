Artist Statement

I am interested in the way we use digital media technologies in order to create systems that bound online and offline spaces together. My works revolve around using these technologies to construct youthful interactions and how it visually tells stories of the current generation. I use Adobe Creative Suite, code, and 3D modeling to display these ideas. Currently, I am exploring how social media affects our own sense of identity. Are we enamored by our physical appearances and constantly thinking about the information we put out? Do photo filters affect our self-views positively or negatively? These are the questions I want to find answers to.
