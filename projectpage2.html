Feed Me Plz_2018

Feed Me Plz is an interactive platform game where the player plays as a mama bird who needs to feed her hungry babies. The player navigates through four seasons in search for food. Beware! There are enemies and obstacles the player has to avoid, such as spikes and moving cats with nets. Unlock the next season by finding the hidden key in a special stage.

Team: William Chung, Emily Cheung, Ru Sia
I designed and created a majority of the visuals and graphics. Feel free to play our game by clicking the splash screen to be directed to our itch.io page.
