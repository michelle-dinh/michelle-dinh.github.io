About_

Michelle Dinh is a Vietnamese-American artist based in San Jose, California. She is currently pursuing her Bachelor of Fine Arts in Digital Media Art at San Jose State University. She enjoys video editing, designing, and drinking milk tea. Much of her work leans towards interactivity and playfulness. With those stylistic choices in mind, she ventures into the spaces of social media to research how it shapes our self-identities. She is interested in learning how younger generations are transforming in response to pop culture. Whether it is through selfies, videos, tweets, and more, Michelle wants to explore those ideas in her work.
