Lost_2019

Lost ​is a 2D motion graphics animation that tells a story of a fish who finds itself in an unfamiliar place. Curious by the rustling sounds nearby, the fish decides to explore and finds himself a mysterious friend. The two friends play a game of chase, however, one gets lost along the way.
